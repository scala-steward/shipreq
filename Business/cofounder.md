Criteria Brainstrom
===================
* Turn a product into a business
* Can pitch well
* Some respectable degree of experience and network
* Plan next steps (eg. get investment THEN 1 yr dev THEN first customers)
* Excited to engage with people
  * leads
  * customers
  * investers
  * community
* Extroverted
* Trustworthy
* Honest (integrity even at the cost of business or profit)
* Reliable
* Argues respectfully, logically, and in good faith.
  Similarly, receptive to such debate from others (me).
* Flexible
* Respectful
* Long-term thinker
* Doesn't get offended easily
* Willing to justify and explain decisions & opinions
* Willing to invest in their own abilities and in the product
  No results = no reward
* Equity will no be handed out immediately.
  It will be handed out slowly in return for results, proportionally respecting
  the years I've already invested and the fact that it's my creative IP in
  addition to just effort-spent.
* Happy to be by-the-book and careful, including involving personal lawyer's in
  partnership decisions
* Knowledge of local business & startup resources
